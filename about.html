Who is Patrice?

Patrice is a Software Engineering student at ÉTS (École de Technologie Supérieure) in Montreal. He likes all kind of programming, but he's particularly passionate about video games development.

Timeline

  • May 2015

    Game Jam Battle

    In May 2015, I participated in the first edition of the Game Jam Battle 44 hours Game Jam with 4 other developers I didn't know beforehand, and we made the game Zoom. The theme was "Two steps forward, one step back".

  • March 2015

    Pixel Challenge Quebec

    In March 2015, 4 other developers and I went to the Pixel Challenge 2015 48 hours Game Jam in order to compete in the Amateur/Student competition, where we got second place with the game À Bout de Souffle. The theme was "L'alliance des forces".

  • September 2014

    Behaviour Interactive

    In 2014, I got my first internship at a video games development company: Behaviour Interactive. I had the chance to work on the game Home: Boov Pop for about 7 months while using the Unity Engine.
